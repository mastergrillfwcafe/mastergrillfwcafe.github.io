Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

We're open Tuesday through Sunday from 4:00 PM to 11:00 PM. We're closed on Mondays for maintenance and restocking.

What type of cuisine do you specialize in?

We specialize in Western cuisine, premium grilled meats, and traditional recipes. Our focus is on authentic charcoal-grilled flavors that bring out the best in every dish.

Do you offer catering services?

Absolutely! We offer superb and affordable catering services including buffet style, pack food, and our famous roasted whole lamb and dome packages that are customer favorites for special events.

What are your most popular catering packages?

Our roasted whole lamb and premium dome packages are the most popular choices for special events. We also offer customizable buffet and pack food options to suit any occasion and budget.

Do you take reservations?

Yes! We highly recommend reservations, especially on weekends. You can call us directly or message us on WhatsApp to reserve your table.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space available for our customers right in front of our restaurant.
